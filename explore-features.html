Comprehensive Features of Spectrum AI

🧠 Cognitive Ability Test

An AI-driven IQ assessment tailored to the individual's age and ASD level, helping to customize the learning approach.

🎭 Interest & Sensory Profile

Guardians provide details about interests, sensitivities, and calming factors to personalize the learning experience.

🎯 Attention Span Checker

Interactive timed games assess an individual’s ability to focus, helping refine teaching strategies.

🖥️ Learning Style Determination

AI classifies learners into visual, auditory, reading/writing, or kinesthetic categories through engaging activities.

📚 Personalized Learning Plan

Based on cognitive assessments and behavior, AI curates a unique curriculum focused on math, language, science, and life skills.

🎮 Adaptive Teaching Strategy

AI continuously adapts lesson plans based on behavioral responses and interests, ensuring an engaging and personalized learning experience.

🌍 Life Skills Training

Through interactive stories and real-world simulations, users develop essential social and emotional skills.

🏫 School-Like Structure

A structured schedule with flexible subject periods to mimic a real school environment, customized to individual learning needs.

🔒 Security & Privacy

Data protection and secure login features ensure the privacy and safety of user information.
